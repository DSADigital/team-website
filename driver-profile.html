About Davi

The Precision Maverick

Born in Goiás, Davi de Sousa's journey in motorsport began at the age of twenty. His transition from Assetto Corsa to the iRacing Formula 1600 category has been marked by a relentless pursuit of aerodynamic perfection and tactical brilliance.

Known for his "risky" racing lines, Davi has earned a reputation for being one of the most efficient drivers in wet conditions. His racing style is a masterclass in controlled aggression—knowing exactly when to push the limits of his #15 machine and when to preserve the rubber for the final sprint.

Away from the track, Davi works closely with DSA Digital's data engineers, translating high-speed telemetry into winning strategies that have redefined the team's performance metrics this season.
